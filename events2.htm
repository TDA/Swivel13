RC CARS

For the car:

  • The car should be powered by electric motor only.
  • More than one electric motor can be used in the car.
  • The chassis of the car has to be designed by the participant.
  • Cars could be controlled with wired or wireless controller.
  • On board power source should be used.
  • Maximum of 12V can be given to the car excluding power supply to the remote.
  • No external power supply is to be used.
  • Tie Breaker round may be conducted if necessary.

For the teams:

  • Each team should consist a maximum of of three participants.
  • Teams should assemble earlier, for inspection of the cars.
  • Cars not meeting the specifications listed above will lead to disqualification.
  • Teams not reaching on time will be disqualified.
  • Any act against the spirit of the game will also lead to disqualification.
For more info, contact : Suresh-9884643988

LINE FOLLOWER:

  • Line Followers must be self-contained and not externally operated by wire or by remote radio control during the race.
  • Except for the battery pack, the handler shall not make any addition, removal, replacement or change to the hardware of a Line Follower during a contest. It is however permissible to make minor repairs.
  • Robots shall not exceed 25 cm in overall length, 25 cm in overall width and 20 cm in overall height.

WATER ROCKET

(Two Participants per team)
  • Bottles will be checked and marked before launch
  • Event will be conducted in 2 rounds
  • 1st will be a prelims followed by a TARGET HITTING finals
  • No materials will be provided for the construction of the rocket.
  • Pump and Launching System will be provided.
  • Capacity of the tank must be 2 ltrs only (Standard Soft Drinks Bottle).
  • Maximum pressure is 70psi and MULTISTAGES are allowed.
  • Only maximum perpendicular distance will be taken into account for prelims, hence minimum deviation is expected.
  • Trails will not be given for rockets.
  • Rocket design will be taken into consideration for final result.
